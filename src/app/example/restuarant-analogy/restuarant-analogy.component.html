Example: NgRx Store Concept — A Restaurant Analogy

Think of the NgRx Store like a Restaurant.

The Store is the Restaurant — the central place where all orders and food preparation happen.

The State is like the kitchen’s order book — it keeps track of all current orders.

Customers (or parts of your application) place orders, but they cannot directly change the kitchen’s order book.

Instead, they send requests (Actions) to the restaurant staff.

The kitchen staff (Reducer) processes these requests and updates the order book immutably — creating a new order list rather than changing the old one.
